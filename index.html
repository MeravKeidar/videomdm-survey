Study title: Human Evaluation of AI-Generated Human Motion

Purpose: This study investigates the perceptual quality of computer-generated human motion animations. Your responses will be used for academic research.

What participation involves: You will watch pairs of short video clips and indicate which appears more realistic. The survey consists of 20 comparisons and takes approximately 2–4 minutes.

Risks and benefits: There are no known risks associated with participation. Your contribution will help advance research in human motion generation.

Confidentiality: No personally identifiable information is collected. All responses are anonymous and will only be used in aggregated form for research purposes.

Voluntary participation: Participation is entirely voluntary. You may stop at any time without penalty.

Compensation: Upon completing the survey you will receive a unique completion code to claim your compensation.
